I Am Fearless

Born to a teenage mother in the ghetto of Philadelphia; more has happened in our lives than I care to admit.

We are walking statistics that beat the odds! Although most choices in life had more to do with making the best of bad situations

we learned to just keep move forward towards our personal goals. So, I am thankful for family, being spiritually grounded and happiness.

My maintance plan includes the practice two actions.

* The first action is to write my emotions in a diary that I only allow myself to read once before placing it in the sea of forgetfullness.

* The second action that I practice is when I feel a energy that is disruptive I practice systematicly implement small changes to

bring about differnt results.


These two actions help me feel unique in my environment and facilitate a sustainable positive plan for lasting constructive change.
